About

Welcome to the world of FETE, where Amapiano rhythms meet pure energy and soul. As a dedicated Amapiano DJ, I bring the authentic sounds of South African house music to dance floors worldwide.


With a deep passion for the genre's signature log drum patterns, jazzy piano melodies, and infectious basslines, every set is crafted to move both body and spirit. From intimate venues to major festivals, the mission is simple: keep the dancefloor alive with the heartbeat of Amapiano.
